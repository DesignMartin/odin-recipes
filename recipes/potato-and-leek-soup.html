Potato and Leek Soup

Description

Potato and Leek Soup is a classic soup that is made with potatoes and leeks. It's a simple dish that is easy to make and tastes great.

Ingredients

  • 5 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 2 leeks, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 litre of chicken broth
  • 2 dl of cream

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the onion, leeks, and garlic and cook until the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the potatoes, bay leaf, salt, pepper, and thyme and cook until the potatoes are softened.
  3. Add the chicken broth and cook until the soup is heated through.
  4. Add the cream and cook until the soup is heated through.
  5. Serve the soup with some bread and butter.
