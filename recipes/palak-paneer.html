Palak Paneer

Description

Palak Paneer is a classic Indian dish that is made with paneer cheese and spinach palak.

Ingredients

  • 200 g of Paneer, cubed
  • 200 g of Spinach, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp of garam masala
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of coriander
  • 1 tsp of turmeric
  • 1 tsp of curry powder
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp of cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp of vinegar
  • 1 tsp of oil
  • 1 tsp of butter

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the onion, tomato, and garlic and cook until the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the paneer and cook until it is browned.
  3. Add the spinach and cook until it is wilted.
  4. Add the garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, curry powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, pepper, sugar, vinegar, and butter and cook until the sauce is heated through.
  5. Serve the palak paneer with some rice and naan.
